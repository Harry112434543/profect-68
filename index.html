Campus Life

School Campus

It was established in 1988

The school campus was inaugurated in 1988 by Sheikh Hamad Bin Saif Al Sharqi, Deputy Ruler of Fujairah. It has an enrollment of 3,250 students from 39 nationalities and around 200 staff members.
